Tier Tree Reasoning
S+ Aspen Aspen trees are the coolest. They look amazing in the fall and their trunks are perfect. Fight me. Oh they also are perfect clones so that's awesome.
S Oak Oaks are a classic tree with no real downsides. They live like everywhere and they're fun to climb.
A Maple Maple trees are kind of like a slightly worse version of Oaks. Some redeeming qualities like helicopter seeds and good fall colors, but not enough to move into S tier
B Cherry These are great during springtime when they are in bloom. The rest of the year they are pretty lackluster.
C Cottonwood Some people like cottonwood trees. Those people are idiots. It's not endearing to shed cotton everywhere, it's annoying! Solid C tier, never going to win tournaments.
F Bradford Pear Absolute trash. You could actually get paid to kill these. Invasive, smell bad, garbage tier.
